About Maarifa Research and Training Associates Limited

Maarifa Research and Training Associates Limited is a non-government and not for profit company that focuses on the provision of research, training and consultancies services to individuals, organisations and government institutions in the fields of education, sexual and reproductive health, early childhood education, psychology and social sciences in general.

Read More

Mission

To promote and support evidence based advocacy and decision making by providing high quality research, consultancy and training services to individuals and institutions in Tanzania and beyond.

Vision

To be a reference point for research, training and professional development services in Tanzania and beyond

Teaching methodology in higher education

“It is no longer enough to have ended at the heights of the ivory tower. Academics are now required to formalise their teaching abilities”, argues Professor Makoni, one of the leading experts in higher education in Africa. Professionalization of teaching in higher education is important in equipping academics with necessary skills to be able to cope with changing dynamics of higher education and the complexities of university classrooms. Indeed, it has been argued that “Good researchers are good teachers”.

Academic writing

Academic writing is at the core of higher education business but there is no formal training to acquire academic writing competencies in most higher education institutions. We provide tailor made academic writing courses to all academic cadres, junior, mid and senior academic staff and other professionals who seek to improve their professional writing and seek to be published. The academic writing courses that we offer include:

Mastering the art and science of academic writing for publication

Writing research proposals for non-academic purposes

Authoring academic and research reports

Writing quality quantitative and qualitative research proposals for academic purposes

Social transformation workshops

  • Consistent with Paul Freire’s critical pedagogy, we inspire and catalyze academics and higher education institutions to maximize the society impact of research and intellectual discourse.

  • We do this by conducting academic and intellectual debates on matters of public interest for purposes of bridging a gap between the academia and the public.
