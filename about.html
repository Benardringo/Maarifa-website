About Maarifa Research and Training Associates Limited

Maarifa Research and Training Associates Limited is a non-government and not for profit company that focuses on the provision of research, training and consultancies services to individuals, organisations and government institutions in the fields of education, sexual and reproductive health, early childhood education, psychology and social sciences in general. Our research approach employs both quantitative and qualitative techniques for purposes of examining the representativeness and generalizability of the research findings, as well as gaining deeper insights of the phenomenon.
The company also aims to facilitate academics and their institutions to position themselves competitively globally, and in producing graduates who are appropriately equipped to function in a competitive professional labour market. We enhance academics’ professional competencies through our expertise training in academic soft skills, research and professional services. We inspire academics and educators to become change agents, and to enhance higher education institutions’ contribution to social transformation.

Our Mission

  • To promote and support evidence based advocacy and decision making by providing high quality research, consultancy and training services to individuals and institutions in Tanzania and beyond

Our Vision

  • To be a reference point for research, training and professional development services in Tanzania and beyond

Objectives

  • To conduct high quality research studies and consultancy services; provide professional training programmes; and organize intellectual debates on matters of public interest.
